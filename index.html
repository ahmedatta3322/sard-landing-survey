What is SARD?

SARD is Oman's first commercial digital streaming platform, built specifically to produce, publish, and grow Omani content — films, series, programs, documentaries, theater, and children's shows.

We believe Omani creativity deserves a platform that truly represents it. A place where our stories are told with pride, and where local producers have a real commercial channel to reach their audience.

By registering your interest, you help us demonstrate real market demand — and bring SARD one step closer to launch.
